    **AquaSentinel** is an automated crayfish habitat monitoring system powered by ESP32. It is designed to maintain optimal water conditions by measuring turbidity and activating a filtration system when needed. The system also monitors water flow and sends notifications in case of irregularities. Additionally, it uses an HX711 load sensor to measure the crayfish’s weight and an infrared (IR) sensor to estimate its length, providing real-time data for efficient habitat management.

    Semester Arc

    WK 01–03
    Foundations. Introduction to rotoscoping, workspace setup, frame extraction, and basic line tracing over still and moving reference.
    WK 04–06
    Form & Light. Line quality studies, colour passes, shadow mapping, and introduction to 3D solar system modeling in Blender.
    WK 07–09
    Hybrid Work. Compositing rotoscoped figures over 3D renders. Motion blur, smear frames, and atmospheric FX layers.
    WK 10–12
    Final Composite. Sound sync, multi-layer compositing, export, and final review. The complete 60-second film is assembled and submitted.
